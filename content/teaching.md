# Teaching

## Syracuse University (2019–present)

**Economics of Emerging Markets**  
An exploration of economic development challenges and opportunities in emerging economies, covering topics such as trade policy, institutional development, and growth dynamics.

**Intermediate Macroeconomics**  
Analysis of aggregate economic behavior including national income determination, inflation, unemployment, monetary and fiscal policy, and economic growth.

**Introductory Macroeconomics**  
Fundamentals of macroeconomic analysis: GDP, inflation, unemployment, monetary policy, fiscal policy, and international trade.

**Introductory Microeconomics**  
Principles of microeconomic theory including supply and demand, consumer behavior, firm production decisions, and market structures.
